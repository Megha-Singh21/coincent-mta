About Us

Coincent is a manage marketplace that offers Live Industrial Training, Projects & Internship to students. We are a professional community of Industry Experts and academia, who have come together to help learners become employable. We use Design Thinking and a learner-centered approach to problem-solving and focusing on applicationbased learning.

Our Industrial Trainers, mentors, and counsellors are passionate tutors & student career-driven specialists in their fields with years of Industrial Expertise in eminent companies like Google, IBM, Microsoft, and more.

Besides we use our leading-edge and comprehensive Internally developed AI tools to make certain that our learners experience customized and personalized learning to achieve exponential success.

We are a bunch of individuals always working to make a difference in students’ career and to meet the needs of the industries by bridging the skill gap between colleges and industries.
